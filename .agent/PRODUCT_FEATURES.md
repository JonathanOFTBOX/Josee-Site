## 🎯 What is Fieldbox?

**Fieldbox** is a mobile-first job management and time tracking app for **field service businesses** — plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, contractors, and any company that dispatches workers to job sites.

**Target Industries:**
- 🔧 Plumbing & HVAC
- ⚡ Electrical contractors
- 🏗️ Construction & Renovation
- 🚚 Delivery & Logistics
- 🛠️ Field maintenance

---

## 👷 For Workers (Mobile App)

### ✅ Job Management
- View all assigned jobs with location, client info, and instructions
- See job priority and estimated hours
- View materials needed for each job
- Access client contact info directly from the app

### ⏱️ Time Tracking (Punch In/Out)
- One-tap punch in when arriving at job site
- GPS verification ensures workers are on-site
- Automatic time calculation with overtime tracking
- Mark jobs as "Complete" or "Need Return Visit"

### 📸 Photo Documentation
- Take **arrival photos** to document site conditions
- Add **work-in-progress photos** during the job
- Capture **completion photos** as proof of work
- All photos are timestamped and GPS-tagged

### 🎤 Voice Notes
- Record voice memos during jobs (hands-free)
- Automatic transcription for easy review
- Perfect for "dirty hands" situations

### 📋 Timesheet
- View personal work history
- See hours worked per week/month
- Track completed jobs and earnings

### 📶 Offline Mode
- **Works without internet** — punch in/out, take photos, add notes
- Automatically syncs when back online
- Never lose data in poor signal areas (warehouses, basements)

## 👔 For Bosses / Admins (Desktop & Mobile)

### 📊 Live Dashboard
- See **all active workers** in real-time
- View who is working on which job right now
- Get alerts for overtime, problems, and check-ins
- Company-wide metrics at a glance

### 📅 Smart Scheduler (Calendar)
- Drag-and-drop calendar scheduling
- Multi-day job spanning with weekend skipping
- See worker workload at a glance
- Create jobs directly from calendar

### 👥 Team Management
- Add workers via email invitation
- Assign workers to trucks/vehicles
- View worker performance and history
- Set hourly rates per worker

### 🧾 Job Lifecycle & Validation
- **To Validate Tab:** Review completed jobs before approval
- Add admin notes and materials to jobs
- Mark jobs as "Completed" or "Needs Return Visit"
- Payment tracking: Validated → Invoiced → Paid

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Track parts and materials in warehouse
- Per-truck inventory (what's on each vehicle)
- Receive stock and track quantities
- Low stock alerts

### 🏢 Multi-Supplier Ordering
- Manage multiple suppliers
- Create purchase orders with one click
- Auto-email orders to suppliers
- Track order status (Pending → Partial → Complete)

### 📈 Reports & Analytics
- Hours worked by employee
- Hours spent per job
- Revenue and profit per job
- Export timesheets to CSV/PDF

### 🔔 Notifications & Alerts
- Real-time push notifications
- Worker check-in alerts (50% completion)
- Overtime warnings
- Job assignment notifications

## 🚚 Fleet Management

### Truck Inventory
- Create and manage company vehicles
- Assign workers to specific trucks
- Track inventory per truck
- QR codes for quick truck identification

### Smart Truck Compatibility
- When creating a job, see which truck has the most materials
- Percentage-based compatibility score
- Minimize warehouse trips

## 💳 Billing & Subscription

### Flexible Pricing
- Per-worker pricing ($49.99 CAD/worker/month)
- Auto-adjusts when workers are added/removed
- 14-day free trial

### Self-Service Billing
- Stripe-powered secure payments
- Manage subscription in-app
- View invoices and payment history

## 🔒 Security & Privacy

- **Row-Level Security (RLS)** — Workers only see their own data
- **Company Isolation** — Each company's data is completely separate
- **Encrypted Storage** — All data encrypted at rest
- **HTTPS Only** — All communications encrypted
