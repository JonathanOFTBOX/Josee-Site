## 💡 Tips for Posting

1. **Personalize it** — Add your own story or frustration
2. **Don't spam** — Share once per major feature, not daily
3. **Reply to comments** — Engage, don't just post and ghost
4. **Add the link** — Put it in the first comment, not the post (better reach)
5. **Use emojis sparingly** — 2-3 max, don't overdo it
